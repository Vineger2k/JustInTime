Our Heritage

FloatFry has been a pioneer in the cookware industry since 1993. What started as a small family business has evolved into a brand known for its commitment to quality and innovation.

Over the years, we have consistently developed unique, family-oriented products, all while maintaining our core values of personalization and exclusivity. Our cookware is designed to meet the highest standards and is oven-safe, dishwasher-safe, and crafted from durable Titanium, making it scratch-resistant.

FloatFry takes pride in its ability to cater to individual customer needs, allowing them to put their signature on our products. This personal touch has made our cookware some of the most prestigious and sought-after in the market.
